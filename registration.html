Registration Information

Please find below the registration details for NPCL² 2026.

Registration is open until 15 July 2026.

1) High-level International Conference

NPCL² 2026 is designed as a small, high-level international conference, with a limited number of participants, not exceeding 50 attendees. The aim is to encourage focused discussions, high-quality exchanges, and close interaction among specialists.

2) No Registration Fee for Participants

No registration fee will be charged for either online or onsite participation. Lunch and dinner during the conference will also be provided free of charge. However, accommodation and hotel expenses will be covered by the participants themselves.

This is made possible thanks to the generous support of the host project “Local Innovations and International Advances in Intelligent Lexicography” [智能辞书编纂的国际前沿与本土创新] (Project Number: H20250649, January 2025 – December 2026), supported by the National Program for Experts in Scientific and Technological Innovation of the People's Republic of China, and by the School of Foreign Languages, Huazhong Agricultural University, China.

3) Hybrid Format

The conference will be held in a hybrid format, both online and onsite. The Zoom link will be sent to registered participants before the conference.
